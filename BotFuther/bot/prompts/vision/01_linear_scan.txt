RIGID LINEAR SCAN PROTOCOL (mandatory — overrides intuitive "whole drawing" reading):

Do NOT analyze the sketch as one undifferentiated picture. Scan the beam axis strictly LEFT → RIGHT (x=0 upward).

## COUNTING PRECISION (no visual guessing)
When locating points on the beam, do NOT rely on visual proportion or quick tick-counting.
Read EVERY number written on the bottom dimension line (1, 3, 1, etc.) and compute **cumulative sums**
to get exact x [m] for each labeled point and support.
If a position is not written as a single number, state it explicitly as a cumulative sum
(e.g. A=0, C=0+1=1, D=1+1=2, E=2+1=3, G=3+3+3=9).

## FOCUS MODE — two-step scan (mandatory)
**Step 1 — Dimension line ONLY:** Scan the bottom dimension chain alone. List every labeled point
(A, C, D, E, G, H, I, B...) with its cumulative x [m]. Do NOT look at load arrows yet.
**Step 2 — Attach loads/supports:** Only AFTER Step 1 is complete, match each load arrow or support
symbol to the labeled point **directly above it** on the beam. Never mix the two steps.
**For EVERY load:** find its center on the beam axis (inclined = arrow tip on beam), drop a vertical to the dimension line — that x is the load position.

## Phase A — Base identification (BLOCKING — complete before any loads)
1) Execute FOCUS MODE Step 1 — dimension chain only → labeled_points + L.
2) Determine total beam length L [m] = sum of ALL segment lengths between tick marks.
3) Locate EVERY support (pin / roller / fixed) and assign exact x [m] from cumulative dimension sums.
4) Do NOT extract or infer loads until L, labeled_points, and all support x-positions are verified.
5) If load-arrow position conflicts with the dimension chain — the DIMENSION CHAIN ALWAYS WINS.

## Phase B — Left-to-right load scan (x=0 → L)
Walk the beam from the leftmost point to the rightmost point. At each station / labeled point:
- Record every distinct load (point, inclined, moment) at that x — if drawn one above the other, each is a separate load.
- UDL: long horizontal line above beam + end arrows; label w is above the line, NOT at a point-arrow tip.
- Point/inclined: magnitude at the top of the arrow.
- For a continuous distributed load: record in distributed_loads[] (start_x, end_x, magnitude, shape) — NOT in loads[].
- Move to the next x station only after the current one is complete.

## Phase C — JSON output order
- List loads[] in left-to-right order of first appearance on the beam (smallest x first).
- Within the same x, order: inclined → point → moment → distributed span starting at x.
